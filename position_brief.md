# Key Concepts Relevant to This Position

## Exposome

The exposome refers to the totality of environmental and social exposures experienced by individuals throughout their lives.

Examples include:

* Air quality
* Green spaces
* Noise exposure
* Crime rates
* Healthcare access
* Education access
* Socioeconomic status
* Neighborhood characteristics
* Environmental risks

The goal is to understand how these factors influence health outcomes, particularly brain health and aging.
